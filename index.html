Made on a stovetop, not in a factory.

Bloomwater started in a kitchen with a mixing bowl, a scale, and four scents that kept getting requested by name. Every bomb is pressed by hand in small batches — usually no more than 40 at a time — so the fizz stays consistent and the color never goes muddy.

No mineral oil, no synthetic glitter, no dye that stains the tub. Just citric acid, baking soda, skin-safe color, and whatever's doing the scent that week.
